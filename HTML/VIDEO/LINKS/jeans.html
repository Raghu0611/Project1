JEANS




DESCRIPTION:When Visu meets Madhumitha (Aishwarya Rai), they quickly fall in love and plan to marry.
But the sweethearts hit a snag in their impending nuptials when Visu's father, Rajamani (Nasser), objects: Visu has a twin brother, Ramu, and the old man is set on both boys marrying twins.
A solution is easily arrived at when Visu introduces his brother to his bride's twin sister -- who is actually Madhumitha in disguise.
Mayhem ensues when Ramu unwittingly falls in love with his future sister-in-law.



Ratings:4/5
